Hummingbird

Date

2017

Project

School

The biggest advantage of industry-led education is that every assignment prepares us to the challenges of a professional brief. This one is one of my favourites: our task was to pick a local Wellington brand, and redesign their logo. I chose a well-known bar and eatery and I decided to take it further by redesigning their whole identity; this includes a menu, coasters, a sign, stationery, and the start of a website template. The logo could also be used on take-away bags, on aprons, or on a chalkboard menu.
