La Princière

Date

2018

Project

School

For this assignment, we were required to pick a small business and redesign their online store. La Princière is a small pâtisserie located in the South of France. Apart from a Facebook page, their online presence is currently inexistent. In addition to the website redesign, I decided to refresh their branding. I chose to keep the branding simple, to reflect the elegance of the bakery. I focused on using luxurious textures and slightly colored paper. The soothing pastel colour scheme is quite common for bakeries, as it resembles the delicate pastries the French are well known for.
