Makeba
Identity

Client: Makeba
Personal project

Makeba is a South African restaurant in Hamilton. The colors of my design are on the country's flag and compliment the colourful food. The pattern is a reference to traditional prints found on South African clothing. My design is vibrant yet extremely simple, so as not to take the focus away from the food. Despite its elegance, it's unpretentious and welcoming.
