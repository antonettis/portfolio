Personal project

Logos are a fundamental part of business marketing. Whether they're for a major corporation or a small business, they are usually the first element that makes customers aware of the brand's existence. The biggest challenge of logo making is asking 'what does the brand represent?' As an young evolving designer, still finding my style, this is a tough one to answer. The process of making this logo helped me realise what kind of designer I wanted to be. The answer? I want to be versatile. I am always looking for new projects, new techniques, new softwares to learn. I like exploring different platforms and media: print, web, video, still images, 3D files, mobile apps... For these reasons I needed a logo that would suit every kind of content. This is the logo design I came up with.
