Soldoa

Date

Project

This flower shop pays tribute to the famous Arabian horse, Sol d'Oa, by wearing his name. In Spanish, 'sol' means 'sun'; the lines beneath the letter O remind of a stylized sunbeam. The deep blue colour paired with the luxurious gold are a reference to traditional Maghrebi clothing.
