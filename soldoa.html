Sol d'Oa

Date

2017

Project

Branding

This flower shop pays tribute to the famous Arabian horse, Sol d'Oa. Florioraphy extends back hundreds of years as a way for people to express emotions and communicate; the intricate flowers illustrations reflect the delicacy and history of such an ancient art. On the other hand, the simple logomark indicates a modern, accessible brand. The colours are inspired by traditional Maghrebi clothing, and the neutral colours will match any bouquet, regardless of the flowers' tones. All of these carefully thought-out elements make the Sol d'Oa experience a memorable one for all of their customers.
